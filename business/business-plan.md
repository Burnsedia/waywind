# Waywind Business Plan

## Product Role
Waywind is an OSS CLI that maps Tailwind/DaisyUI themes to Waybar CSS for Linux desktop theming workflows.

## ICP
- Primary: Linux/Hyprland power users and frontend developers.
- Secondary: OSS theming creators and dotfiles maintainers.

## FOSS and Monetization Boundary
- Free forever: CLI core, base conversions, standard outputs.
- Paid: curated preset packs, setup consulting, sponsorship perks.

## Business Model
- OSS utility grows audience through npm and GitHub.
- Revenue comes from support, curated assets, and sponsorship.

## Primary KPI
- Monthly active installs.

## 90-Day Objectives
- Improve install-to-first-success path.
- Publish preset examples and benchmarks.
- Convert first sponsor/support customers.
